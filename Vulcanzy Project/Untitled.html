VULCANZY

Sore High

The fest was diligently organized. There was a soul to it. A soul that was vibrant, contagious and exuberant. At the entrance of the main block were the huge banners denoting the VULCANZY 2K18. The tone of decorations was very festive as you entered the main block of NIT-Andhra Pradesh. Prof. Y.N.Reddy of NIT-Warangal arrived in time along with the guests Prof. Srinivasa Rao of NIT-Andhra Pradesh to the inaugural ceremony and they were received by the fest coordinator Dr. Ahmed.The students welcomed them by presenting blossoming bouquets of flowers and sang the prayer song which is a custom tradition. The inaugural ceremony was filled with all the association and club heads who were enthusiastic and set the mood for the celebrations. Soon they declared the festival open with the lighting of the lamp. Then the respected dignitaries gave their valuable messages, experiences and ideas in the form of speeches to encourage students on taking active participation in the fest.
